About Us

Mindy and Mike, newlyweds with a passion for sustainability, founded NourishTogether to combat food waste and provide nourishing meals to the community. Our "Pay As You Feel" model ensures everyone can access fresh, nutritious food, regardless of their financial situation.

We work with local supermarkets and distributors to divert food that would otherwise go to waste, turning it into delicious meals served at our restaurant and caf√©. Join us in making a positive impact on our community and the environment.
